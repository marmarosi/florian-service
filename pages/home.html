Star Wars Information Services

Movie Characters

Display the characters of a movie using the following URL:

/characters/<title>

For example:

A New Hope
The Empire Strikes Back
Return of the Jedi
The Phantom Menace
Attack of the Clones
Revenge of the Sith
The Force Awakens

Common Characters

Display the characters that play in both movies using the following URL:

/matchingCharacters/<title1>/<title2>

For example:

A New Hope & Return of the Jedi
The Phantom Menace & Attack of the Clones
Revenge of the Sith & The Force Awakens

Tall Characters

Display the characters with a minimal height using the following URL:

/tall=<height>

For example:

230 cm tall characters
220 cm tall characters
210 cm tall characters
200 cm tall characters
